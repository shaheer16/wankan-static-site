About Us

Welcome to wankan karate club. Feel your unique culture in our dojo; our team builds confidence and self-esteem on the journey to black belt and beyond. Train in a friendly environment with professional instructors who teach defensive and offensive techniques so you can protect yourself and your loved ones. We train in a modern, comfortable, and safe facility. We also include weapons training as part of our black belt syllabus—covering footwork, stances, blocks and strikes—as well as how to use everyday objects defensively.

All Ages
